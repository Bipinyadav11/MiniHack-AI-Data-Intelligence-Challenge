# MiniHack: AI Data Intelligence Challenge

## Overview
This project analyzes a Last-Mile Delivery dataset containing 2,080 delivery records across 10 Indian cities. The objective is to identify key factors affecting delivery delays and provide actionable operational recommendations.

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- SciPy

## Problem Statements

### Q1: Peak Hour Delay Analysis
Analyzed whether orders placed during peak hours (8–10 AM and 5–8 PM) experience higher delays compared to off-peak hours.

### Q2: Weather vs Delay Correlation
Studied the impact of different weather conditions (Clear, Rain, Fog) on delivery delays and identified the most affected order type.

### Q3: Rider Experience Effect
Performed statistical testing to determine whether rider experience significantly impacts delivery delays.

### Q4: City-Level Performance Dashboard
Created performance insights based on:
- City-wise on-time delivery rate
- Monthly delay trend
- Vehicle type comparison

## Key Findings
- Peak-hour orders experience significantly higher delays.
- Fog and Rain contribute to the highest delivery delays.
- Rider experience has no statistically significant impact on delays.
- Operational improvements should focus on peak-hour demand and adverse weather conditions.
